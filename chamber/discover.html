GENERAL INFORMATION

TOURIST ACTIVITIES

Ubication

It is located 20 kilometers from Bogotá and is characterized by its easy access (Calle 80 - Autopista Medellín).

Short History

It is the youngest municipality in Cundinamarca, founded in 1903, called Jardín de la Sabana (Garden of the Savannah) for its high production and export of flowers.

General Data

It's ideal climate has made it one of the richest and most productive areas in the agroindustrial sector. Potato, pea, corn, and carrot crops are some of the highest-quality products produced in these lands.
